# Mentoria Hub — Pitch Deck Outline & Demo Script

Use this as the script for your 5–8 slides + ≤4-minute video. Each slide maps to
a judging criterion (weights in brackets).

---

## Slide 1 — Title
**Mentoria Hub** · "Every opportunity. Every course. One Hub."
Team name · Round 1 — Working MVP Challenge.

## Slide 2 — The Problem  *(Problem understanding — 20%)*
- Mentoria runs on **Telegram + live Zoom calls** → doesn't scale as it grows.
- Students **miss live sessions** (school, exams, time zones, internet).
- Opportunities (olympiads, scholarships, summer schools) are **scattered** across
  sites, channels and docs.
- Students can't tell **what fits their age, interests and goals**.
- Mentoria needs a scalable system that isn't manual Telegram updates.

## Slide 3 — The Solution
One platform, two pillars:
1. **Opportunity Catalog** — everything in one filterable place.
2. **Async Learning** — self-paced courses you take anytime, no live call needed.
Plus an **AI counselor**, **gamification**, and an **admin panel** so Mentoria
scales content without engineers.

## Slide 4 — Target audience
Grades 8–11 in Kazakhstan & globally, prepping for top universities — seeking
STEM / Business / Social Impact opportunities and SAT/IELTS/subject prep.

## Slide 5 — Key features (with demo screenshots)  *(MVP functionality — 25%)*
- Smart onboarding → personalized recommendations
- Catalog with filters, search, save, deadline countdowns
- Course player: syllabus, video, materials, auto-graded quiz, progress bar
- Dashboard: XP, streak, saved items, urgent deadlines
- Admin: real-time add/edit/delete of opportunities & courses

## Slide 6 — Innovation  *(Innovation & creativity — 15%)*
AI College Counselor · XP + streaks + leaderboard · Grade 9–12 roadmap builder ·
trilingual (EN/RU/KK) · dark mode · simulated Telegram deadline alerts.

## Slide 7 — Impact for Mentoria  *(Impact — 20%; UX/Design — 20% shown throughout)*
- **Scales beyond Telegram** — content managed via admin, not manual posts.
- **Retention** — gamification + progress tracking keep students returning.
- **Professional image** — premium UI impresses partners, schools, sponsors.
- **Accessible** — async + multilingual reaches students who can't attend live.

## Slide 8 — Tech & roadmap
Next.js 14 · React · Tailwind · Framer Motion · typed localStorage store (DB-ready).
**Next:** Supabase auth + Postgres, real video hosting, email/Telegram bot,
mentor portal, certificates.

## 🎬 4-minute demo script (the exact user journey judges want to see)
1. **Open** Mentoria Hub → onboarding modal: enter name, grade 11, pick **Business** + **University Admission**.
2. Land on home → **recommendations** are now personalized.
3. Go to **Opportunities** → filter to *Business*, search, **save** the Wharton competition, **Apply** (watch the **+50 XP** toast).
4. Open **Academic English** course → play a lesson → take the **mini-quiz** → pass → **progress bar** jumps, XP awarded.
5. Open **Dashboard** → show streak, saved items, **urgent deadlines**, continue-learning.
6. Toggle **language** (RU/KK) and **dark mode**; open the **AI counselor** and ask "any urgent deadlines?".
7. Show the **Roadmap** (grade 9–12 milestones) and **Leaderboard**.
8. Switch to **Admin** → add a new opportunity → it appears **instantly** in the catalog. End on impact statement.
